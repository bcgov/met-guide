The **Image Widget** allows you to display an image on your engagement page along with an optional description. This widget is ideal for showcasing photos, infographics, or any visual content that can enhance your engagement and provide additional context to your audience.

After an Administrator creates an engagement, Administrators and Team Members can add widgets to display on the engagement page. For more information on widgets in general, please see the [Widgets page](/met-guide/posts/widgets/).

![Image Widget as seen in the widget list](/assets/UserGuideImages/Images/image-widget/image-widget-admin-listing.png){: .light .shadow .rounded-10}

After selecting **Image**, you can add a description (optional) and upload an image. The image must be in JPG, PNG, GIF, or WEBP format and should not exceed 5MB in size. Images under 1MB are recommended for optimal performance on slower internet connections.

![Image Widget form as seen on the admin side](/assets/UserGuideImages/Images/image-widget/image-widget-admin-form.png){: .light .shadow .rounded-10}

After uploading, you may also crop your image using the "crop" button to ensure its best display on the engagement page. The image will be displayed at a 2:3 aspect ratio, so cropping can help ensure that the most important parts of the image are visible.

![The crop view of the Image Widget](/assets/UserGuideImages/Images/image-widget/image-widget-crop-view.png){: .light .shadow .rounded-10}

It is also recommended to add an image description (alt text) for accessibility purposes, as this description will be read by screen readers for visually impaired users or in situations where the image cannot be loaded. The description should be concise and accurately describe the content of the image.
